Building Backgrounds

Fall in love with creating a home for your characters.

Adding characters to an environment is an important topic to understand and add to every artists work flow.

This course focuses on teaching the art of character drawing in their surroundings. The instructor covers topics such as perspective, idea development, and character interaction to enhance the thought process of the students.

The class is suitable for anyone looking to improve their thinking skills during the initial stages of creating art.

In this class you will learn:

Fundamentals of perspective and why it's important

Understanding your character in perspective

The horizon line and how it affects your character

Why it's important to know the eye level in your drawing

How to design a room with 1 point perspective

How to make your characters live in their environment

Layering landscapes in perspective

Making perspective believable and using perspective to to tell a story
