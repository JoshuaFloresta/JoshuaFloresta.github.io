Who are we?

Since 2023, VSArt has offered professional illustration classes online, taught by experienced artists.

John Doe, Anna Lee, and William Berners came together to create instructional videos that provide an inside look into their professional illustration processes. In 2014, they were joined by Lee White, and together they have built a valuable resource for anyone interested in art and illustration. SVSLearn hires only the best working professionals to teach, ensuring students receive top-quality instruction.

John Doe, Anna Lee, and William Berners

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to inspire and encourage artists to pursue their creative passions and guide them through the art world. We believe that every artist has the potential to create something truly unique and meaningful, and that pursuing hobbies that make them happy is a crucial part of the creative process.

Our goal is to provide resources and support to artists at every stage of their journey, from beginners just starting out to seasoned professionals looking to expand their skills. We offer a range of educational materials, from online courses to workshops and mentorship programs, to help artists develop their craft and achieve their goals.

We believe that creativity is a powerful force that can change the world, and we are committed to empowering artists to use their talents to make a positive impact. Our community is inclusive and supportive, and we are dedicated to creating a safe and welcoming space for artists of all backgrounds and skill levels.

Whether you are just starting out or looking to take your art to the next level, we are here to support you on your journey. Let us help you unleash your creativity and bring your artistic vision to life.
